Thursday Evening at Lord's
Lord's will again be the place to be on Thursday nights when the NatWest T20 Blast returns this summer.
England One-Day captain Eoin Morgan will be hoping to continue his recent fine form, and will be joined by New Zealanders Brendon McCullum and Tim Southee in a side packed full of international experience.
First up is the 'London Derby' on Thursday 13th July when Middlesex take on rivals Surrey. Tickets for the game are selling quickly, so please book early to avoid disappointment.
